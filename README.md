# DrumCircle

DrumCircle is an innovative mobile app designed to revolutionize the way users learn and practice drumming. With a community driven library of sheet music exercises, DrumCircle allows users to seamlessly navigate through different levels of rhythm reading. Each exercise is accompanied by clear, precise audio cues, enabling users to practice confidently and effectively. Ready to put your skills to the test? Simply hit the record button, perform the exercise, and receive instant feedback on your tempo and rhythmic accuracy. DrumCircle doesn't just score your performance; it also provides detailed data visualizations that track your progress over time, helping you to improve and gain confidence. Join our vibrant community-based learning platform and embark on your rhythmic journey today!
